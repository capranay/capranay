About

Beyond the Balance Sheet

I'm a Chartered Accountant and Company Secretary working at the intersection of finance, law, and business strategy. Over the last 9+ years, I've built a practice around one idea: compliance shouldn't be a source of stress for the people running a business — it should be handled quietly, correctly, and on time.

That practice now spans 1000+ clients across 8+ countries, from Indian founders incorporating entities abroad to overseas businesses that need dependable accounting and tax support in India. I've filed 8000+ income tax returns and handled 350+ registrations — company incorporations, GST, and secretarial filings — for individuals, startups, and established firms alike.

Based in India, I work closely with founders who need a finance partner that understands both sides of a cross-border setup — the paperwork, and the deadlines that come with it.
